About GoFindit

Every day, valuable items go missing on campus—from backpacks and keys to wallets and ID cards. GoFindit was designed to simplify the process of reuniting these items with their owners. Our platform uses AI-powered text-based matching to suggest potential matches between lost and found items. Whether you’ve lost something or found something, GoFindit provides a safe space to report and recover.
